Pricing

Non-profit organizatons can enjoy all the benefits of a Gold Memberships free of charge when they join with us.

Bronze

$200/year

  • Inclusion in Chamber Directory
  • Access to Monthly Networking Events
  • Exclusive Member Discounts for Chamber events and services
  • Access to Members-Only Resources
  • Quarterly Newsletter Subscription with business tips and Chamber updates
  • Access to Basic Business Development Workshops (online)

Silver

$500/year

  • All Bronze Membership Features
  • Priority Business Listing in Directory with logo
  • Complimentary Admission to 2 Networking Events per year
  • Free or Discounted Access to a variety of Chamber-hosted seminars and workshops
  • Discounted Sponsorship Opportunities for Chamber events (up to 10% off)
  • Become Part of the rotation of Business Spotlight on Homepage
  • Exclusive Invitations to Member-Only roundtable discussions or forums
  • Voting Rights in Chamber elections

Gold

$1000/year

  • All Silver Membership Features
  • Premier Business Listing on the Chamber website with logo and direct link to your website
  • Unlimited Complimentary Admission to all Networking Events
  • Free Entry to All Chamber Workshops and access to special events
  • Exclusive Sponsorship Opportunities
  • Access to Chamber Leadership for strategic advice and support
  • VIP Invitations to networking mixers, galas, and other special events
  • Priority Consideration for Chamber awards and recognitions
