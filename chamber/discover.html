Anaconda History

Nestled in the heart of the beautiful Deer Lodge Valley, Anaconda was founded in 1883 during the peak of the copper mining boom. It became the headquarters of the Anaconda Copper Mining Company, one of the largest copper producers in the world. Today, the town embraces its rich industrial heritage while offering a welcoming community and scenic landscapes. Visitors can explore its historic architecture, enjoy outdoor recreation in the surrounding mountains and rivers, and experience the charm of a town that played a pivotal role in Montana’s development.
