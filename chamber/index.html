Upcoming Events

  • December 7 - Anaconda Christmas Stroll

    The Anaconda Christmas Stroll is an annual holiday event where Anaconda's Main Street comes alive with festive lights, holiday decorations, and the sounds of carolers. Local shops stay open late for special deals, and the streets are filled with holiday cheer, including a visit from Santa Claus.

  • December 20 - Anaconda Winter Festival

    Held at Washoe park, the festival is a fun, active event for all ages. With holiday-themed contests and crafts like ice-carving, snowman building, and ornament creation, your sure to bring something home for grandma. Local vendors serve up warm treats like hot cocoa and roasted chestnuts.
