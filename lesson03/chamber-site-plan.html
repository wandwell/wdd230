Great Falls Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Purpose

We are a local non profit focused on strengthening our community by promoting local businesses, hosting community events, and building human connections. We know that a united community can make a difference in the world and seek to give our members a voice in government on both a city and state level.

Scenarios

  • What community events are occuring this month and how will this help my business?
  • Who can I contact to become a member?
  • How does the the chamber of commerce promote local businesses?
